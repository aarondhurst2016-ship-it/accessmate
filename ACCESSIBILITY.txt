Talkback Assistant Accessibility Information

- Fully screen reader compatible (desktop and mobile)
- High contrast and large font options available
- Keyboard navigation and focus highlighting
- Speech feedback for all major actions and errors
- Accessible error handling: all errors are spoken and displayed
- Tooltips and help sheet for all controls
- Voice command and speech-to-text input supported
- Customizable accessibility profiles

For support or suggestions, contact: support@talkbackassistant.com
